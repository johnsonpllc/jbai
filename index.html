Questions

Frequently Asked

Still unsure? Chat with Vex or reach out directly — we'll answer anything.

Contact Us →
How many leads is my business actually losing right now? +
More than most owners realize. Studies show that 62% of calls to small businesses go unanswered, and 85% of callers who can't reach a business on the first try never call back. Source: Invoca ↗. If your business receives 50 calls a month and misses 30, and your average job or booking is worth $200 — that's potentially $6,000/month walking out the door.
What happens to website visitors who come after hours? +
Without an AI Chatbot, they leave — and most won't come back. Research shows that 82% of consumers expect an immediate response when they have a question. Source: Salesforce ↗. An AI Chatbot greets every visitor, captures their information, and answers their questions — 24/7, including weekends and holidays.
How long does it take to get my AI Chatbot or AI Voice Bot live? +
Most AI Chatbots are live within 3–5 business days. AI Voice Bots typically take 5–7 days to set up, train, and test. We don't launch until you're happy with how it sounds and responds — and we're in Orange County, so we can meet in person if needed.
Do I need technical knowledge to get started? +
None at all. We handle everything — building, training, installing, and maintaining your AI. You describe how your business works and what questions customers ask. We take care of the rest and walk you through each step.
What kinds of questions can the AI Chatbot answer? +
Anything you train it on — and we do the training. Hours of operation, holiday schedules, pricing, services offered, booking availability, directions, FAQs, and more. For anything outside its scope, it captures the visitor's contact info and notifies you so you can follow up.
Can the AI Voice Bot sound like a real person? +
Yes. We use natural-sounding voices and write scripts that match your business personality. Most callers won't realize they're speaking with an AI. Many of our clients choose to be upfront about it — and customers actually appreciate the honesty.
What's the ROI — does this actually pay for itself? +
For most service businesses, yes — within the first month. If an AI Chatbot captures just 5 leads per month that you would have otherwise missed, and your average customer is worth $300, that's $1,500 in new revenue against a $99/mo maintenance cost. Businesses using AI customer service report an average 3.5x ROI per dollar spent. Source: Forbes ↗
Will AI replace my employees? +
No. AI handles the repetitive, time-sensitive tasks — answering the same questions, capturing leads after hours, taking bookings by phone — so your team can focus on the work that actually requires a human. Your people handle relationships. AI handles the routine.
