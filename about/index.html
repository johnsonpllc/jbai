We're two brothers from Orange County who believe small businesses deserve enterprise-grade AI tools — with real people behind them.

Our Story

Founded in 2026 in Orange County, CA, Johnson Brothers AI is a homegrown company built on a simple belief — local businesses deserve the same powerful technology that enterprise companies use, with someone actually in their corner to help them use it.

We work with small businesses across Southern California to drive real growth through AI Chatbots, AI Voice Bots, and modern websites that convert visitors to customers. While most digital agencies operate remotely and hands-off, we're boots on the ground — available in person and invested in your success.

"In an era of faceless digital services, we believe local businesses deserve a local partner — someone you can actually call, meet, and count on."

That's what sets JB.AI apart. We're not a platform, a dashboard, or a ticket system. We're your neighbors, and we treat every client like one.
