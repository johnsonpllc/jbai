This Week's Tip

Main Tip

Your restaurant's website might look great, but small friction points are quietly driving away hungry customers before they book a table.

• Make your reservation button impossible to miss – Place it prominently above the fold and repeat it after your menu section where appetite peaks.

• Speed kills conversions – If your site takes longer than 3 seconds to load, you're losing customers to competitors. Google

• Mobile-first or mobile-last – 60% of restaurant searches happen on phones, so your booking flow must work flawlessly on small screens.

• Cut the clicks – Every extra step in your reservation process costs you bookings. Aim for maximum 3 clicks from homepage to confirmed reservation.

• Show, don't just tell – High-quality food photos and real customer reviews near your booking button increase conversion rates by up to 25%.
