This Week's Tip

Here's a quick framework to measure your AI chatbot's ROI and prove its value to your local service business.

  • **Track after-hours inquiries** - Monitor how many leads come in outside business hours (nights, weekends, holidays) that your chatbot captures versus what you'd miss with just voicemail or contact forms.

  • **Calculate lead value** - Multiply your average customer lifetime value by your typical conversion rate. If you close 20% of leads at $500 average value, each lead is worth $100.

  • **Measure response time impact** - Instant chatbot responses can increase conversion rates by 30-50% compared to next-day callbacks. [CallRail](https://www.callrail.com/blog/lead-response-time-study) found that responding within 5 minutes makes you 9x more likely to convert.

  • **Factor in labor savings** - Calculate hours saved on initial lead qualification and appointment scheduling, typically 2-3 hours per week for small service businesses.

  • **Simple ROI formula** - (Additional monthly revenue + labor savings - chatbot cost) ÷ chatbot cost × 100 = ROI percentage.
