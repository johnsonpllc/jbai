This Week's Tip

Here's your 10-minute method to calculate if an AI chatbot will pay for itself at your local service business.

  • **Track your current after-hours missed calls** - Check your phone logs for calls between 6 PM-8 AM and weekends. Most local service businesses miss 40-60% of after-hours inquiries that turn into competitor wins.

  • **Estimate conversion value** - Multiply missed calls by your average service ticket and close rate. If you miss 20 calls weekly at a 25% close rate with $300 average jobs, that's $1,500 in lost monthly revenue.

  • **Calculate chatbot capture rate** - AI chatbots typically capture 70-80% of after-hours leads through instant responses and appointment booking. Apply this percentage to your missed opportunity total.

  • **Factor in monthly costs** - Subtract your chatbot subscription ($100-500/month) from captured revenue. Most local service businesses see 300-500% ROI within 90 days.

  • **Add voice bot coverage** - AI voice bots handle overflow calls during busy periods, typically adding 15-25% more lead capture on top of chat.
