This Week's Tip

Here's how to quickly calculate whether an AI chatbot will pay for itself at your local service business.

  • **Track your missed calls**: Check your phone logs for after-hours and weekend calls you couldn't answer—multiply by your average job value to see potential lost revenue.

  • **Calculate lead capture improvement**: AI chatbots typically convert 30-40% of after-hours inquiries into qualified leads versus 0% when no one answers [HubSpot](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/chatbot-marketing-stats).

  • **Factor in response time advantage**: Businesses that respond within 5 minutes are 9x more likely to convert leads [InsideSales](https://www.insidesales.com/insider/lead-response/).

  • **Estimate monthly gains**: If you miss 20 calls monthly worth $200 each, capturing just 30% equals $1,200 in new revenue.

  • **Compare to chatbot costs**: Most AI chatbot solutions run $50-300/month—if you capture even 2-3 extra jobs monthly, you're profitable.
