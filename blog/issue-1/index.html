This Week's Tip

**Main Tip**

Here's a simple 10-minute framework to calculate your AI chatbot's ROI and prove its value to your local service business.

  • **Track after-hours inquiries**: Monitor how many leads come in outside business hours (typically 40-60% of web traffic occurs after 5 PM). Multiply by your average conversion rate to see missed opportunities.

  • **Calculate lead capture value**: Take your average job value × conversion rate × number of after-hours contacts your chatbot handles monthly. This gives you monthly revenue potential.

  • **Factor in response speed**: [Harvard Business Review](https://hbr.org/2011/03/the-short-life-of-online-sales-leads) found companies are 7x more likely to qualify leads when responding within an hour versus waiting longer.

  • **Subtract your costs**: Include chatbot subscription fees, setup time, and maintenance hours at your hourly rate.

  • **Compare to hiring staff**: Calculate the cost of extending phone coverage 24/7 versus your AI solution—typically 80-90% savings.
