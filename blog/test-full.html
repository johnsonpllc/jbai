This Week's Tip

Here's a quick framework to calculate your AI chatbot's ROI and see real impact on your bottom line.

  • **Track after-hours inquiries**: Count missed calls and website visitors outside business hours for one week. Multiply by your industry's average conversion rate (typically 2-5% for local services).

  • **Calculate potential monthly revenue**: Take those missed opportunities × your average customer value. A plumber missing just 10 after-hours calls weekly could lose $12,000+ monthly.

  • **Factor in lead quality improvement**: AI chatbots qualify leads 24/7, so your team focuses on ready-to-buy prospects. This typically increases conversion rates by 20-30%.

  • **Subtract chatbot costs**: Most AI voice/chat solutions run $200-800 monthly. Compare this against your potential monthly revenue loss.

  • **Add operational savings**: Calculate time saved on repetitive inquiries. AI handles 70% of common questions, freeing staff for higher-value work.

ROI often pays back within 30-60 days for most local service businesses.
