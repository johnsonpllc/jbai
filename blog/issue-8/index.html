This Week's Tip

Here are three AI tools that can reclaim 8+ hours of your week without replacing a single team member.

• Inbox triage with SaneBox ($7/month) - Automatically sorts your email by importance and moves low-priority messages to a separate folder you review weekly instead of daily.

• Meeting notes with Otter.ai (free tier available) - Records, transcribes, and creates action items from your calls so you can stay present instead of frantically taking notes.

• Follow-up drafts with Grammarly GO ($12/month) - Generates personalized email responses based on context, tone, and your writing style in seconds rather than minutes.

• The compound effect - Users report saving 45-90 minutes daily when combining all three tools, according to Zapier's automation survey.

• Start small - Pick the tool that addresses your biggest time drain first, then add the others once you've built the habit.
