This Week's Tip

## Main Tip: Calculate Your Missed Lead Costs to Justify AI Voice Bot Investment

Every unanswered call represents real money walking out the door—here's how to quantify that loss and see why AI voice bots pay for themselves.

  • **Track your missed calls for one week** using call logs or phone system reports. Multiply by 52 to get your annual missed call volume.

  • **Calculate your lead conversion rate** by dividing closed deals by total leads from the past quarter. Most small businesses convert 2-5% of leads into customers.

  • **Determine average customer value** by looking at your typical sale amount plus any repeat business over 12 months.

  • **Apply the formula: Missed Calls × Conversion Rate × Average Customer Value = Annual Lost Revenue.** A restaurant missing 10 calls daily at 3% conversion with $50 average orders loses $27,375 annually.

  • **Compare this loss to AI voice bot costs** (typically $200-500/month) to see your ROI potential.
