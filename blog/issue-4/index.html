🎯 Main Tip

Missing after-hours calls is costing local businesses an average of $62,000 annually in lost revenue, but AI voice bots can capture every opportunity while maintaining that human touch.

  • Start with a warm greeting – Program your bot to answer with your business name and a friendly "How can I help you today?" instead of robotic menu options.
  • Use natural conversation flow – Modern AI can handle interruptions and follow-up questions just like your best employee would.
  • Capture appointment bookings instantly – Set up direct calendar integration so customers can schedule services without waiting for callbacks.
  • Qualify leads with smart questions – Train your bot to ask the right questions upfront, then route urgent calls to your emergency line.
  • Follow up within 2 hours – Even with AI handling initial contact, personal follow-up calls during business hours seal the deal.
  • Test weekly with mystery calls – Regular testing ensures your bot sounds natural and captures all necessary information.
