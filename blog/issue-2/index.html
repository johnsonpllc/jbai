This Week's Tip

Here's how to quantify what those missed calls are actually costing your business.

  • **Track your missed call volume** – Most phone systems show missed calls, but dig deeper into peak hours and patterns to understand when you're losing the most opportunities.

  • **Calculate your lead-to-customer conversion rate** – If 20% of leads typically convert, and your average sale is $500, each missed call potentially costs you $100 in lost revenue.

  • **Factor in lifetime customer value** – A single missed call could mean losing a customer worth thousands over multiple years, not just one transaction.

  • **Monitor competitor response times** – While you're missing calls, competitors with AI voicebots are capturing leads 24/7, even outside business hours.

  • **Audit your current coverage gaps** – Identify specific times when calls go unanswered – lunch breaks, after hours, busy periods – to see your biggest leak points.
