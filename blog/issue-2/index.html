This Week's Tip

Here's how to calculate what those missed calls are actually costing your business.

  • **Track your missed call volume** – Check your phone system logs or ask your team to count unanswered calls for one week, then multiply by 52 for your annual total.

  • **Estimate your conversion rate** – Most local service businesses convert 20-30% of callers into paying customers, so use 25% as your baseline calculation.

  • **Calculate average job value** – Add up your last 20 jobs and divide by 20 to get your average customer value per transaction.

  • **Do the math** – Multiply missed calls × conversion rate × average job value. A plumber missing 5 calls daily at $300 average jobs loses $113,750 annually.

  • **Factor in lifetime value** – Happy customers typically return 2-3 times, so multiply your total by 2.5 for the real impact.

  • **Consider AI voice bots** – They answer every call 24/7, converting missed opportunities into booked appointments automatically.
