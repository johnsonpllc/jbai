This Week's Tip

Here's your quick formula to prove AI chatbot ROI for local service businesses.

  • **Track after-hours inquiries**: Monitor how many leads contact you outside business hours through your website, social media, or missed calls. Most local businesses lose 60-80% of after-hours opportunities.

  • **Calculate current lead value**: Multiply your average monthly leads by conversion rate, then by average customer value. Example: 20 leads × 15% conversion × $500 = $1,500 monthly revenue.

  • **Estimate chatbot capture rate**: AI chatbots typically capture 40-60% of after-hours inquiries that would otherwise be lost. Voice bots can handle overflow calls during busy periods.

  • **Factor in 24/7 availability**: Unlike staff, AI works weekends and holidays when emergency service demand peaks. Calculate the revenue from just 2-3 additional monthly conversions.

  • **Subtract chatbot costs**: Most solutions run $50-200/month. If you capture even one extra customer monthly, ROI is typically 300-500%.
