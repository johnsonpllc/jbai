This Week's Tip

## Main Tip

Here's why a chatbot might be your small business's new best friend.

  • **24/7 coverage without overtime pay** – Your chatbot never sleeps, gets sick, or takes vacation days, handling customer questions around the clock while you focus on growing your business.

  • **Handle the repetitive stuff instantly** – About 80% of customer questions are basic inquiries like hours, pricing, or return policies that chatbots can answer in seconds.

  • **Free up your time for what matters** – Instead of spending hours answering the same questions, you can focus on product development, sales calls, or actually running your business.

  • **Scale without hiring immediately** – As your customer base grows, your chatbot handles the increased volume without needing to hire additional support staff right away.

  • **Collect valuable customer data** – Every interaction gives you insights into what customers really want and where they get stuck in your process.
