This Week's Tip

Most small businesses rush to automate lead gen — but the bigger win is hiding in your inbox.

  • Your time leaks start with repetitive questions. "What are your hours?" and "Do you offer refunds?" are costing you hours every week.
  • FAQ automation runs 24/7 without you. A simple AI chatbot or auto-responder handles these instantly, even at 2am.
  • It builds trust before a sale happens. Customers who get fast answers convert better — Drift found response speed directly impacts buying decisions.
  • It's the easiest automation to set up. No complex integrations — just document your top 10 questions and plug them in.
  • Sales automation needs a warm audience. FAQ automation creates one.

Start simple. Start useful. Start here.
