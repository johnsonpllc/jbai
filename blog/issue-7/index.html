This Week's Tip

Text-based AI reminders are quietly revolutionizing how small dental practices handle appointment no-shows, cutting them by up to 30%.

• Perfect timing matters most — Send your first reminder 48 hours ahead, then a final one 2-4 hours before the appointment when patients are planning their day.

• Keep messages conversational, not clinical — "Hi Sarah, just a friendly reminder about your cleaning tomorrow at 2pm with Dr. Johnson!" beats sterile appointment confirmations every time.

• Make rescheduling effortless — Include a simple reply option like "Reply RESCHEDULE if you need to change this appointment" so patients don't just ghost you.

• Personalize beyond the name — Reference their specific treatment ("your crown fitting" vs "your appointment") to make it feel less automated.

• Track your wins — Most practice management systems show before/after no-show rates, proving ROI to justify the monthly AI tool cost.
